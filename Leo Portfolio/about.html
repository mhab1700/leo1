About Leo

My name is Leonardo Sanchez, I was diagnosed with autism back when I was in high school. Drawing has helped me with stress, anxiety, and depression. My favorite show is Steven Universe, this show has motivated me to draw better and create new characters. Video games have also played a big part in my drawing techniques. One of my biggest influences in video games has been Battlefield 3, I incorporated both Steven Universe and Battlefield 3 when creating my own guns and characters.
